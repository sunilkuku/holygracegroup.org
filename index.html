Welcome to Holy Grace Group

The Holy Grace Group was established in 1999 under the banner 'Holy Grace Foundation' a registered charitable and educational society formed with the intention of molding next generation professionals with a global edge Holy Grace Academy is an English medium co-educational CBSE Residential School founded in 2000 with 135 students, 15 staff on roll and has consistently grown into a big family of around 3000 students and 300 staff as on today. With a view to making education friendly and interesting to the first time school goers, the School has designed a Smart Kindergarten with latest technologies and innovative ideas to match international standards. This is a comprehensive academic schedule for Pre-Kg, LKG and UKG which reduces the burden on children, teachers and parents and makes learning a stimulating and rewarding experience.

The Holy Grace Academy of Management Studies impart quality education with a global edge in the area of management and research. It is the only Institute in South India accredited with IAO, USA. It is also accredited with ACBSP, USA. It is ranked among 75 best MBA colleges in India. It is AICTE approved and Calicut University affiliated and also A-rated (by the Central Government accreditation statutory body, All India Management Association) The Holy Grace Academy of Engineering offers a complete industry endorsed package with the university syllabus at its core and all that the industry needs strategically bound around as super competency add-on modules. The program offers specialization in Civil, Computer Science, Electronics & Communication and Electrical & Electronics with faculty from IIT, NIIT and renowned universities in India. We provide industrial visits abroad and within the country.

Why Holy Grace Group ?

  • Placement Cell

    Experienced professionals from Industry and Academia are paart of the Placement Assistance team.The team is committed to deliver the best to the students and mould them as industry ready professionals.
  • Modern Lab

    The laboratories are well advanced with the premium equipments which facilitate the students of Holy Grace to experiment, learn and analyze in order to develop the academic and practical know-how.
  • International Tour

    We offer International tour program to gain a global perspective and to imbibe the global cultures which are essential skills for interacting in a multi-cultural, multi-linguistic environment.
  • Digital Library

    Our Library is well equipped with rich collections of above 13000 books covering all disciplines of engineering and technology, science, humanities and management.
  • Sports

    We emphasize the importance of sports, gym, yoga etc. in achieving physical and emotional health which will equip the students to cop up with the pressure of studies. We also have swimming pool, air rifle academy and horse riding training in our campus.
  • Wi-Fi Campus

    The entire campus is Wi-Fi enabled with a high speed internet connection to allow the students to access the internet no-matter wherever they are. The coverage of Wi-Fi is not only restricted to class rooms but also extends to all the areas including library, Auditorium, cafeteria, and hostels.
  • Transportation

    We provide transportation facilities to the nearby places /towns
  • College Hostel

    Our hostels are within the campus with an accommodation facility for 150 boys and 300 girls. Warden/Matron and resident tutors are appointed to look after the discipline.
